PROJECT 4

Inspire

This poster is very inspiring to me and also expresses a concept that I want to convey. When we do a reasonable sort of garbage, our planet will become more and more pure.

Idea

Contemporary people use computers almost every day, and we also remove unwanted files into the trash of the computer. So my thoughts come from this, putting the garbage in my life into the designated garbage bin reasonably. Following the spirit of Yama-ichi and paying tribute to the online culture, I hope my theme can be associated with them.

Mini Waste sorting bin

When I made these bins, I also collected a lot of production methods, and finally chose to use cardboard to make it is also an environmental concept. I hope that through this project, people around me can make a lot of essential items by using waste. The colors and icons of these trash cans are the same as in reality. It is also reminding that this is not just an ornament, but also a garbage classification in real life.

Icon

The use of cartoon icons can first attract people's attention to add interest so that people can pay attention to this matter from different angles. Including adults can also teach children how to do garbage classification, just like a game will form a good habit from an early age.

Final

Although the final presentation is not particularly refined, I think it makes sense. The materials used to make this work are almost all discarded. What I want to convey is that any abandoned item can be reused to make new things.
